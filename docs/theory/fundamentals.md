EQT is based on Planck’s formula:
![Planck Formula](https://latex.codecogs.com/png.latex?\dpi{100}E=h\nu)
Particles are sorted by frequency:
- <10²⁰ Hz: Photons, neutrinos
- 10²⁰–10²⁴ Hz: Electrons, protons
- >10²⁴ Hz: Higgs, gravitons
